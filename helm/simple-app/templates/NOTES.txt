1. Check the Helm release:
   helm list

2. Check the Deployment and Pods:
   kubectl get deployments
   kubectl get pods

3. Access the service locally:
   kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "simple-app.fullname" . }} 4000:{{ .Values.service.port }}

4. Open the health endpoint:
   http://127.0.0.1:4000/health
